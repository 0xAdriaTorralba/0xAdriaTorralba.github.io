ZisK zkVM — Binary & Main circuits review

 OpenZeppelin

ZisK (0xPolygonHermez/zisk) · Nov 2025

Scope: PIL2 constraints for the ZisK zkVM: binary opcode circuits (binary.pil, binary_add.pil, binary_extension.pil, binary_extension_table.pil, binary_table.pil) and the main execution trace (main.pil)

Security review of the core PIL2 circuits of the ZisK general-purpose zkVM. Focused on the correctness of the constraint system for binary opcodes (MIN, MAX, LT, GT, EQ, ADD, SUB, LE, AND, OR, XOR, SLL, SRL, SRA, SE, …) and on the main execution-trace constraints that stitch segments together and enforce control flow.
