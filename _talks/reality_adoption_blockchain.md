## Abstract

This talk addresses the real state of blockchain technology adoption beyond the hype. It examines where the technology stands in terms of maturity, what the practical and proven use cases are, and where the gap lies between marketing promises and actual deployed systems.

The presentation provides a grounded perspective on what blockchain technology can and cannot do today, and discusses the trajectory of adoption across different sectors.
